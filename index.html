Clasic Modern Design

Classic modern design, also known as mid-century modern design, emerged in the mid-20th century and is characterized by simplicity, functionality, and an emphasis on clean lines and organic forms. Influenced by the Bauhaus movement and Scandinavian design principles, classic modern design features minimalist aesthetics, open spaces, and a focus on functionality over ornamentation.
